# Business Foundation Module - Dixon Smart Repair

## Overview
This directory contains all Business Foundation artifacts that establish the business context, requirements, and justification for the Dixon Smart Repair project.

## Purpose
The Business Foundation module creates essential business context that will drive:
- Product Planning decisions
- Architecture & Design choices  
- Development Implementation priorities
- Infrastructure & Deployment requirements

## Directory Structure
- `stakeholder-interviews/` - Individual stakeholder interview files and analysis
- `research/` - Business research, market analysis, and competitive intelligence
- `decisions/` - Key business decisions made during the foundation phase

## Artifacts in This Module
This module will contain business artifacts that provide the foundation for all subsequent development work.
